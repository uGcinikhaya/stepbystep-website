MY DATA SCIENCE JOURNEY

About My Journey And Why I Chose This Career

My name is Gcinikhaya Mthethwa, i've had quite a bumpy and very eventful journey. In my high schooling career i wasn't really sure of what i wanted to become professionally, so that indirectly lead to me being a very underwhelming performer, when it came to academics. Graduated from High School with a Diploma National Senior certificate, which limited what i would be able to choose from once applying for different career paths. I then tried my hand in many things such as, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering which even then i was not passionate about, so my performance was never what i could regard as my best. The Year 2016 a shift occured in my life that required me to stop thinking i have time to fool around and be a kid. the following year in 2017 my mother passed away and that left me in a very stressful state, and to make it out it needed me to grow up and be a man, even though i had just turned 20. I got a job and fortunately got a learnership to become a Clinical Pathology Medical Technician, the duration began in January 2020 and i passed my board exam in November 2021. It is the year 2023 now and i seek to do something more with my life, i want to become a Data Analyst(Scientist), i've learnt a bit of Python, Java, HTML and CSS. I now seek to focus solely on Python and SQL in order to dedicate myself to this new commitment i have made. I am 26 years old and Bootcamps seem to be my only way to learn these languages and better prepare myself for the next phase of my life.
